EcoSense - Safeguarding Waterways with Cutting-Edge AI

Our Mission

EcoSense is committed to safeguarding waterways with cutting-edge AI, using predictive models and machine learning data analysis for high quality data collection for our Iwi and Aotearoa.

Key Benefits

  • Cost-effective Compliance: Reduce compliance burdens with simplified reporting and real-time insights.
  • Empowered Farmers: Enhance productivity while protecting water resources.
  • Community Engagement: Foster collaboration among stakeholders with comprehensive, culturally-informed data.

Our Solutions

Real-Time Monitoring: Track water quality metrics instantly, allowing for rapid response to potential issues.

Predictive Analytics: Use AI-driven models to forecast water quality trends, helping to plan proactively.

Collaborative Platform: Bring together stakeholders on a single platform for shared insights and coordinated action.

Empowering Farmers

EcoSense offers farmers AI-powered tools to monitor their impact, reduce environmental risks, and contribute to the national effort to restore New Zealand’s waterways.

Our Vision

We believe in a future where technology and tradition come together to preserve New Zealand’s natural heritage, combining AI with indigenous knowledge and modern farming techniques.
